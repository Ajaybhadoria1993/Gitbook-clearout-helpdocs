## What Clearout Adds to Your Platform

### Email Verification (Real-Time + Bulk)

Clearout verifies emails with deep checks including:

* Syntax + domain validation
* MX + SMTP verification
* Disposable and role-based detection
* Spam trap risk signals
* Deliverability protection

Your users get cleaner lists, fewer bounces, and better inbox placement.

### Email Finder + Contact Enrichment

Turn incomplete records into full prospect-ready profiles.

* Find verified B2B emails from names + domains
* Enrich leads with attributes for routing and personalization.
* Power smarter segmentation and outbound targeting

Your platform becomes a prospecting engine, not just a database.

### Prospecting Workspace + Reverse Lookup

Enable GTM teams to build targeted lead lists directly inside your product:

* Filter by role, industry, location, and company data
* Reverse lookup from emails or LinkedIn URLs
* Push enriched leads into CRMs and sales workflows instantly

Outbound becomes signal-driven, not guesswork.

### Form Guard (Forms + Websites)

Prevent low-quality leads from being captured.

Clearout Form Guard validates:

* Email addresses
* Phone numbers
* Names (gibberish + spam patterns)
* Disposable and bot-driven submissions

This ensures only real, conversion-ready leads reach your users’ CRM.

### Phone Validation

Verify phone numbers in bulk or in real time:

* Format and validity checks
* Line type (mobile/landline/VoIP)
* Carrier + geo intelligence
* Time zone support for outreach timing

It is ideal for managing SMS, WhatsApp, call workflows, and signup forms.
