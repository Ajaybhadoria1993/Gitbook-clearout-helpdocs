# Gravity

Use Clearout with **Gravity Forms** to validate email addresses in real time and stop fake, disposable, or invalid signups before they reach your lists or CRM.&#x20;

Install the [Clearout Email Validator plugin](https://wordpress.org/plugins/clearout-email-validator/) for WordPress and connect it to your Clearout account to start validating submissions from your Gravity Forms instantly.
