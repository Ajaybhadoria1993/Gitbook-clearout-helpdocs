# Team Account

## Apps & Integrations

Connect **Clearout** with your favorite tools to verify emails, clean lists, and find leads directly inside your existing workflow.\
With our growing collection of integrations, you can keep your contact data accurate — everywhere you work.

***

### What You Can Do

&#x20;\- Verify emails **directly inside your CRM** (HubSpot, Zoho, Salesforce)\
\- Automate email cleaning via **Zapier**, **Pabbly**, or **Make (Integromat)**\
\- Enrich sign-ups and leads through **WordPress** or **Google Sheets** plugins\
\- Use **API & Webhooks** for full developer control

***

### Automate Your Workflow

#### Example Automation (Zapier)

* **Trigger:** New subscriber in ConvertKit
* **Action:** Verify email via Clearout
* **Result:** Add only valid leads to your CRM automatically

```yaml
Workflow Example:
ConvertKit → Clearout → HubSpot
```

#### Example: Google Sheets Add-on

* Install **Clearout for Google Sheets**
* Select your email column
* Click **Verify Now** → Get instant results in adjacent columns

***

### Security & Authorization

All apps connect through **secure OAuth 2.0** or **API key authorization**, ensuring:

* Encrypted data transfers
* Read-only access where applicable
* Full GDPR & CCPA compliance

***

### Integration Tips

&#x20;Always test the integration with a few sample records before going live.\
&#x20;Use **Bulk Verifier** for large data syncs.\
&#x20;Enable email notifications for integration success/failure alerts.\
&#x20;Monitor API usage under **Dashboard → API Analytics**.
